My name is Brianna Montes I am from Texas and I have a degree in CIT. I have taken coding classes and I have experience with different types of coding. My career is working with computers and I have been working with computers since college. My company can help design and make your website more presentable. I am wanting to make your website catch everyone’s attention who visits your website. When was the last time you visited a website that caught your attention? I can make your website do exactly what you want it to do and I can make your website look exactly how you want it to look! I can make your future design possible!

Purpose Statement

My purpose for this website is to make it easy to be able to navigate around the website. I am wanting the website to run smoothly without any complications. I am wanting for my customers to be able to find my website easy, and to be able to find what they’re looking for easily. I want this website to have the right amounts of headings and links to navigate to my social media accounts. My plan for the customers is to catch their attention on my website by having colors and pictures that will draw their attention to my website.

Audience Statement

My target customer is New Tech Building Maintenance, Inc. This is a janitorial company for commercial, the company first started in the year 2000. It began in a small town in Laredo, Tx. It then grew to Dallas, Tx and the company also started doing construction work. This company is wanting to grow in Texas and for their needs to seek more customers browsing on their website. My plan is to catch my audience my meeting their needs with any janitorial services and construction work they need. This website is going to stand out from the others with my designs. My janitorial services and construction work is my top service to my customers that they will love.

Misson Statement

New Tech Building Maintenance, Inc. It started in the 2000 and is still going. This company first started small with commercial cleaning and has grown and now has added construction work. New Tech is helping commercial buildings by providing for their needs in any construction work they want. The company New Tech provides for their employees and with full benefits. We help our customers and employees and give them the best experience by working with us and being our customer. It is important to this company to satisfy our customers and to keep our employees happy.
